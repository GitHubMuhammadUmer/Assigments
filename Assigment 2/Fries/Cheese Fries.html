Cheese Fries

Place a piece of aluminum foil over a baking sheet; spread fries in a single layer on baking sheet. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown and crisp, 15 to 20 minutes. Preheat broiler. Sprinkle fries with cheese and crumbled bacon. Broil until cheese is melted, 1 to 2 minutes. Serve with Ranch dressing.
