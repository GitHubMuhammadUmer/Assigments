Chicken Malai Boti

Malai means cream and boti means bites of chicken. Chicken Malai Boti gets its name from its extremely tender, melt-in-the mouth texture. It is also sometimes referred to as Malai Chicken Tikkaor Murgh Malai Tikka. It's a luscious Pakistani BBQ delicacy made with chicken marinated in a cream and yogurt sauce.
