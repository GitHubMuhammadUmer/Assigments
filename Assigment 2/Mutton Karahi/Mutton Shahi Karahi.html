Mutton Shahi Karahi

Shahi Mutton Karahi Recipe is a scrumptious way to cook karahi with goat meat. The main ingredient of shahi mutton karahi is almonds which gives it a royal taste. The almonds can be added in various forms i.e. roasted, soaked, crushes or blended. If you like karahi recipes, this shahi karahi are a must-try. The juicy shahi mutton karahi is very traditional.s
