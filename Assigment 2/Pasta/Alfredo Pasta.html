Alfredo Pasta

Pasta Alfredo is just a richer version of this. In a large pot of boiling salted water cook the fettuccine al dente. in a medium pan add 1/2 the butter, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese and 1/2 -1 ladle of hot reserved pasta water (about 3-5 tablespoons). On low medium heat, simmer and whisk together to make a cream.
