Mutton Biryani

Mutton biryani in awadhi or lucknowi style- a rice and meat dish that is known for its heady aroma and fragrant spices. First soak the basmati rice after three-four wash. Soak it for 30 minutes. You can make ahead this. Thinly sliced a large onion. After cutting the onion, open the slices with your hand. This will ease the caramelization process.
