Pakistan


Urdu and English are Pakistan's official languages
All government documents, many street signs, many shop signs, business contracts and other activities use English.
The language of the courts is also English. English is taught to all school-level Pakistani students, and in many cases the medium of instruction is also in English.


Pakistan is bounded by Iran to the west, Afghanistan to the northwest and north, China to the northeast, and India to the east and southeast The coast of the Arabian Sea forms its southern border
